Maguro (Bluefin Tuna)
- Parts:
    - kashiraniku (top of head)
    - kama/kamatoro (gill flesh)
    - otoro (fatty part) -> between harakami (abdomen near gills) and haranaka (center of abdomen)
    - chutoro (back and stomach)
        - sekami: part of back close to head
        - senaka: center of back
        - seshimo: part closest to tail
        - harashimo: part of haranaka close to tail 
    - akami: center of tuna's body

- Types:
    - honmaguro: northern bluefin
    - minamimaguro: southern bluefin
    - mebachimaguro: bigeye
    - kihadamaguro: yellowfin
    - binnagamaguro: albacore
    -> Ahi (Yellowfin & Bigeye)
